Features

Everything you need to manage billing, orders, and your kitchen — in one free platform.

Billing & Orders

Billing & Order Management

Create professional, GST-compliant invoices in seconds. Manage dine-in, takeaway, and delivery orders from a single screen. Split bills, apply discounts, and track payments — all without switching between tools.

Handles multiple payment modes including cash, UPI, and card payments. Checkout is fast and error-free.

Menu

Menu Management

Add, edit, and organise your entire menu in minutes. Create categories like Starters, Mains, Beverages, and Desserts. Set prices, add variants (half/full, small/large), and mark items as vegetarian or non-vegetarian.

Changes reflect instantly across all devices and your billing screen — no restart, no delay.

Kitchen

KOT / Kitchen Display System

When an order is placed, a Kitchen Order Ticket (KOT) is automatically sent to your kitchen display or printer. Your kitchen staff sees exactly what to prepare, with table numbers, item names, and special instructions.

Eliminates handwritten notes, reduces mistakes, and speeds up service during peak hours.

Inventory

Inventory Tracking

Track your raw materials and ingredients at the item level. Set minimum stock thresholds and receive alerts when supplies are running low. Link ingredients to menu items so stock is automatically deducted with every sale.

Reduce food waste, plan purchasing better, and keep costs under control.

Staff

Captain / Staff App

Your waitstaff can take orders tableside using any mobile phone or tablet. Select the table, pick items from the menu, add notes, and submit — the order goes directly to the kitchen and billing counter.

No more running back and forth. Faster service, fewer errors, happier customers.

Cloud

Cloud Storage

All your bills, reports, menu data, and customer records are stored securely in the cloud. Access everything from any device, from anywhere. Your data is automatically backed up.

Whether you are at your restaurant or at home, your business data is always a click away.

Devices

Works on Any Device

PeeledOnion is a web-based application that works on any device with a browser — Android phones, iPhones, iPads, tablets, laptops, and desktops. There is nothing to download or install.

Use the devices you already have. No specialised POS hardware required.
