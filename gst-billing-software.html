FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this GST billing software really free?

Yes. PeeledOnion offers lifetime free GST billing software for Indian restaurants with no hidden charges.

Does it calculate CGST and SGST separately?

Yes. PeeledOnion GST billing software automatically splits GST into CGST, SGST for intra-state and IGST for inter-state transactions.

Can I add my GSTIN to the invoices?

Yes. You can add your GSTIN, business name, and address in settings. They will appear on every invoice generated by PeeledOnion.

Does it support multiple GST rates?

Yes. PeeledOnion supports 5%, 12%, 18%, 28% and custom GST rates. You can set different rates for different menu categories.

Does PeeledOnion GST billing software support HSN codes?

Yes. You can assign HSN/SAC codes to your menu items. These codes will automatically appear on your GST invoices as required by Indian tax law.
