No. The free plan will always remain free. Optional paid add-ons may be introduced for advanced features, but you are never required to upgrade.

No. You can create unlimited bills and invoices on the free plan. There are no usage caps.

No. Sign up and start using PeeledOnion immediately without entering any credit card or payment information.
